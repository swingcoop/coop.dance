How to join us

Potluck is a community collaboration. There are many ways to participate, and here are four:

Dance

Drop in! Classes are by donation.

Share

Bring a snack or a jazz step to share.

Invite

Help other people participate by inviting them to join us.

Teach

Share a small idea in 10 minutes, or a bigger idea in an hour.

Questions?

Get on the schedule

Share something you love about dancing.

The idea with Potluck is to be a community space to share and dance with each other.

If you'd like to teach or share something – now or in the future – please sign up. We're always putting together the next series, and we'd love if you can be a part of it.

Solo jazz warmup

Play one song and dance to it while people in class copy what you do. 3-5 minutes.

Jazz step

Pick a favorite jazz step of yours, or find one on YouTube, and break it down for the class. 2-4 minutes.

Small idea

Share a concept that made a difference in your dancing, or maybe a fun thing you did in another workshop, or a partner-dance move. 10 minutes of teaching, plus two songs for practice – so about 20 minutes.

Main lesson

A prepared presentation on a primary dance concept or routine. One hour.

Full class

Share the thing in an hour and a half. Includes a snack break.

Snacks

Instead of dancing, snacks.
