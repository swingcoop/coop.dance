Partner Charleston, 1930s

The Charleston, once the world's biggest dance craze, is fun, easy to pick up, and the perfect way to start swing dancing, with or without a dance partner.

This class is suitable for anyone who wants to have a good time, and has no prerequisite for attendance, though a reservation is required.

Mondays: 7-8:30 p.m.

Fun with Lindy hop

Lindy hop, often called the original swing dance, started in the 1920s in Harlem, and today is danced around the globe. It is a fun and expressive dance rooted in old jazz and having a good time.

This class is suitable for most people reading this. To attend, we ask that you've been to at least one swing dance (anywhere) in the past year.
