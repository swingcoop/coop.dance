Swing Dance Starter Kit

Welcome to the world of swing dancing. With Swing Dance Starter Kit, you'll learn the essentials to start having fun on the dance floor in as little time as possible — only three weeks.

This class is free — it's a six-lesson series that will introduce you to social dancing, Lindy hop, Charleston, and East Coast swing.

Photo: Abigail Stuart House, November 3

Our goal in this series is to get you comfortable on the dance floor, and help you get started dancing on a regular basis — in Olympia, Tacoma, Seattle, Portland, Bellingham, and anywhere there are dances hosted by Lindy hoppers around the world.

Requirements

This class series is for beginners and experts. Intermediate dancers are welcome, but they will likely have the hardest time in this series, as most of their time will be spent unlearning what they have learned.

To be fair to all students, the first two sessions, December 3rd and 5th, cannot be missed.
